About Us

RedefinIT is an IT Software startup founded in 2021 with the goal of redefining and simplifying the management and automation of bare-metal server infrastructure. The company is headquartered in Bengaluru, India. The flagship product BMA is API-driven tool that can deploy OS and Firmware on bare-metal servers.
